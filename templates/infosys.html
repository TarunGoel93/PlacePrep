About Infosys

Infosys Limited is a global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting, headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Founded in 1981, it provides IT services, business consulting, and outsourcing solutions to clients across industries like financial services, healthcare, and retail. Infosys is known for its focus on innovation, sustainability, and employee development.

Infosys Recruitment Process

1. Application and Screening

  • Apply via the Infosys careers portal, campus recruitment drives, or platforms like InfyTQ.
  • Ensure your resume highlights technical skills, projects, and certifications.
  • Referrals from current Infosys employees can enhance your application.

2. Interview Rounds

Infosys’ hiring process is structured to assess technical proficiency, aptitude, and cultural alignment.

Online Assessment (OA)

  • Conducted on platforms like InfyTQ or third-party tools (e.g., HackerRank).
  • Sections: Aptitude (quantitative, logical reasoning, verbal), Technical MCQs (DSA, DBMS, Programming), and Coding (1-3 problems).
  • Focus areas: Arrays, Strings, Basic Algorithms, Puzzles.

Technical Interview (1-2 rounds)

  • Each round lasts 30-45 minutes, conducted virtually or onsite.
  • Topics: Programming (C, Java, Python), DSA, DBMS, OS, OOP.
  • Questions on coding, problem-solving, and project experience.

HR Interview

  • Evaluates communication skills, team fit, and career aspirations.
  • Discusses role expectations, location preferences, and compensation.

Infosys Interview Preparation

Preparation Tips

  • Practice LeetCode Easy and Medium problems, emphasizing arrays, strings, and sorting.
  • Study DBMS (SQL queries, joins) and OS (scheduling, memory management).
  • Prepare for aptitude tests using resources like PrepInsta or RS Aggarwal.
  • Improve coding fluency in a preferred language (Java, Python, or C++).
  • Showcase collaboration and learning agility to align with Infosys’ values.

Common Interview Questions

Technical Questions:

  • Write a program to find the factorial of a number (iterative and recursive).
  • Create a SQL query to join two tables and retrieve specific data.
  • Implement a queue using two stacks.
  • Sort an array using QuickSort and explain its time complexity.
  • Explain inheritance in OOP with a coding example.

HR Questions:

  • Describe a situation where you demonstrated teamwork.
  • Why do you want to work at Infosys?
  • How do you handle pressure in a fast-paced environment?
  • What are your long-term career goals?
