About Wipro

Wipro Limited is a leading global IT, consulting, and business process services company headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Founded in 1945, Wipro provides digital transformation, cloud services, and IT solutions to clients across industries like banking, healthcare, and energy. Known for its innovation and client-centric approach, Wipro is a prominent player in the IT services sector.

Wipro Recruitment Process

1. Application and Screening

  • Apply through the Wipro careers portal, campus recruitment drives, or platforms like Elite National Talent Hunt.
  • Update your resume with relevant technical skills, projects, and certifications.
  • Referrals from current Wipro employees can strengthen your application.

2. Interview Rounds

Wipro’s hiring process evaluates technical skills, aptitude, and cultural fit through a structured series of rounds.

Online Assessment (OA)

  • Conducted on platforms like AMCAT or Wipro’s proprietary systems.
  • Sections: Aptitude (quantitative, logical, verbal), Coding (1-2 problems), and Written Communication (essay writing).
  • Focus areas: Arrays, Strings, Sorting, Basic Algorithms.

Technical Interview (1-2 rounds)

  • Each round lasts 30-45 minutes, conducted virtually or onsite.
  • Topics: Programming (C, Java, Python), DSA, DBMS, OS, Networking.
  • Expect coding problems, technical questions, and discussions on project experience.

HR Interview

  • Assesses communication skills, team fit, and career goals.
  • Discusses role expectations, location preferences, and compensation.

Wipro Interview Preparation

Preparation Tips

  • Practice LeetCode Easy and Medium problems, focusing on arrays, strings, and basic algorithms.
  • Study DBMS (SQL queries, normalization) and OS (processes, memory management).
  • Prepare for aptitude tests using resources like PrepInsta or IndiaBIX.
  • Improve coding proficiency in a preferred language (C, Java, or Python).
  • Demonstrate adaptability and client focus to align with Wipro’s culture.

Common Interview Questions

Technical Questions:

  • Write a program to check if a number is prime.
  • Write a SQL query to find duplicate records in a table.
  • Implement a stack using an array.
  • Sort an array using Bubble Sort and explain its time complexity.
  • Explain polymorphism in OOP with a coding example.

HR Questions:

  • Tell me about a time you overcame a challenge in a project.
  • Why do you want to join Wipro?
  • How do you handle conflicting priorities in a team setting?
  • What are your short-term career goals?
