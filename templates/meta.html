About Meta

Meta Platforms, Inc., headquartered in Menlo Park, California, is a global technology company focused on social media, connectivity, and virtual reality. Formerly known as Facebook, Meta is renowned for products like Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Oculus, and is one of the Big Five tech companies, emphasizing innovation in social networking and the metaverse.

Meta Recruitment Process

1. Application and Screening

  • Apply through the Meta Careers portal, campus recruitment, or platforms like LinkedIn.
  • Tailor your resume to highlight technical projects, scalability experience, and relevant skills.
  • Referrals from Meta employees can significantly enhance your application.

2. Interview Rounds

Meta’s hiring process is rigorous, focusing on technical skills, problem-solving, and alignment with its mission to connect people.

Phone Screen

  • Initial 30-45 minute call with an engineer.
  • Includes 1-2 coding problems (algorithms, data structures) and brief behavioral questions.

Online Assessment (Optional)

  • Hosted on platforms like HackerRank or CoderPad.
  • Typically includes 2-3 coding problems focusing on algorithms, data structures, and optimization.
  • Emphasis on clean code and edge cases.

Technical Interviews (3-5 rounds)

  • Each round lasts 45-60 minutes, conducted virtually or onsite.
  • Topics: Data Structures (Arrays, Trees, Graphs), Algorithms, System Design, Object-Oriented Programming, Meta-specific technologies (e.g., React, GraphQL).
  • Expect whiteboard coding, system design (e.g., scalable social platforms), and behavioral questions tied to Meta’s values like impact and collaboration.

Final Interview

  • Conducted by a senior engineer or manager to assess technical depth and cultural fit.
  • May include cross-functional questions to evaluate adaptability.

Hiring Decision

  • Feedback from all rounds is reviewed by a hiring committee.
  • Final discussions cover role, team fit, and compensation.

Meta Interview Preparation

Preparation Tips

  • Practice LeetCode Medium and Hard problems, focusing on graphs, dynamic programming, and string manipulation.
  • Study system design (e.g., designing social media platforms, APIs) and Meta-specific technologies (e.g., React, GraphQL).
  • Prepare behavioral responses using the STAR method, emphasizing impact, collaboration, and adaptability.
  • Use platforms like Interviewing.io or LeetCode Premium for mock interviews.
  • Practice explaining algorithm optimization and handling edge cases clearly.

Common Interview Questions

Technical Questions:

  • Design a news feed system for a social media platform.
  • Implement a graph traversal algorithm to find mutual friends.
  • Find the shortest path in a weighted graph.
  • Optimize a string compression algorithm.
  • Explain sharding in distributed databases.

Behavioral Questions:

  • Tell me about a time you made a significant impact on a project.
  • Describe a situation where you collaborated with a challenging teammate.
  • How did you handle a project with ambiguous requirements?
  • Why do you want to work at Meta?
