About X

X Corp, headquartered in San Francisco, California, is a technology company focused on building a global platform for communication and information sharing. Formerly known as Twitter, Inc., X aims to accelerate human scientific discovery and advance collective understanding. With a presence in multiple countries, X is known for its innovative approach to real-time communication and data processing.

X Recruitment Process

1. Application and Screening

  • Apply through the X Careers portal, campus recruitment, or platforms like LinkedIn.
  • Optimize your resume to highlight technical projects, scalable systems, and relevant skills.
  • Referrals from X employees can significantly boost your application.

2. Interview Rounds

X’s hiring process emphasizes technical expertise, problem-solving, and alignment with its mission to advance human understanding through open communication.

Phone Screen

  • Initial 30-45 minute call with a recruiter or engineer.
  • Includes technical questions (coding, system design) and behavioral questions to assess cultural fit.

Online Assessment (Optional)

  • Hosted on platforms like HackerRank or CoderPad.
  • Typically includes 2-3 coding problems focusing on algorithms, data structures, and optimization.
  • Emphasis on clean code and edge cases.

Technical Interviews (3-5 rounds)

  • Each round lasts 45-60 minutes, conducted virtually or onsite.
  • Topics: Data Structures (Arrays, Trees, Graphs), Algorithms, System Design, X-specific technologies (e.g., real-time data processing, social media platforms).
  • Expect whiteboard coding, system design (e.g., scalable messaging system), and behavioral questions tied to X’s mission of transparency and innovation.

Final Interview

  • Conducted by a senior engineer or manager to evaluate technical depth and cultural alignment.
  • May include cross-functional questions to assess adaptability.

Hiring Decision

  • Feedback from all rounds is reviewed by a hiring committee.
  • Final discussions cover role, team fit, and compensation.

X Interview Preparation

Preparation Tips

  • Practice LeetCode Medium and Hard problems, focusing on graphs, dynamic programming, and real-time data processing.
  • Study system design (e.g., designing scalable messaging or notification systems) and X-specific technologies (e.g., Kafka, Redis).
  • Prepare behavioral responses using the STAR method, emphasizing innovation, transparency, and user focus.
  • Use platforms like Interviewing.io or LeetCode Premium for mock interviews.
  • Review time-space complexity and practice explaining solutions clearly.

Common Interview Questions

Technical Questions:

  • Design a scalable notification system for real-time updates.
  • Implement a rate-limiting algorithm for API requests.
  • Find the top K frequent words in a stream of posts.
  • Optimize a distributed cache for user data.
  • Explain event-driven architecture in real-time systems.

Behavioral Questions:

  • Tell me about a time you innovated to solve a technical challenge.
  • Describe a situation where you ensured transparency in a project.
  • How did you handle a disagreement with a teammate over a technical decision?
  • Why do you want to work at X?
